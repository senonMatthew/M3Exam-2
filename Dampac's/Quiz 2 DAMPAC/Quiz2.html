The Child From Cotabato City

Life

Hello! I am Mohaimen Dampac but I do much prefer being called as Idan (EEDAN). I was born in Davao City but was raised in Digos for 3 years. My dad wanted to sell our house for a long time so after it got sold, we then eventually moved to Cotabato City where we stayed there till I was in Grade 4 in Notre Dame of Cotabato Elementary School. When I hit Grade 5, we moved back to Davao City where we pretty much just settled down. My Parents enrolled me to Davao Chong Hua High School back then was known as Davao Central High School where I graduated Elementary and High School. My time there was a roller coaster, it was fun the first few years but the latter years didn't end so well so I enrolled to University of Mindanao where I finished Senior High and after that I immediately enrolled to Malayan Colleges Mindanao to pursue a career in Programming.

Spare Time

On my spare time, I like to take care of my body first and I'll always prioritize that. I would go to the gym daily and if I'm in the mood, I would go for an early morning jog. When I'm finished with taking care of my body, I would just sit in front of my computer and play video games or watch any movie or series till I'm done with the day. I used to be a DotA player and was on my way to becoming a professional esport player but sadly, it didn't happen because of my dad was not supportive of it so I stopped playing and would only play if a friend of mine would invite me for a couple of games.

My hobby is using the computer because ever since I was 10 years old, I was already infront of the monitor playing Vice City, GTA San Andreas, etc. Right now, I would either play games, watch anything that interests me or do coding till I'm tired enough to go to bed and sleep.
